Suspected IIX Jakarta Traffic Outage — October 30 2025

Date observed: October 30 2025 | Locator: IIX Jakarta (Indonesia Internet Exchange) | Status: Under investigation
Monitoring panels from IIX Jakarta indicate a large-scale traffic collapse across multiple peers between approximately 01:30 and 02:30 local time. Traffic for some nodes remained near zero until around 08:00. While no official outage confirmation has been published, the data suggests a significant disruption at exchange-level.

Observed Evidence

Below are two key screenshots from the IIX monitoring dashboard showing peer-throughput behaviour:

In the first image, the "Huawei Cloud – Arista / 136907" peer line falls sharply to near-zero at roughly the 1:30–2:30 window and remains suppressed until ~08:00. The second image shows dozens of peers following similar patterns.

Potential Impact

  • The Jakarta exchange is a major regional hub: disruptions here could affect many Indonesian ISPs and content providers.
  • Prolonged outage (>6 hours) increases risk of traffic rerouting via alternate paths (e.g., Singapore) with higher latency and costs.
  • Downstream services — content delivery, cloud access, VPNs — could face severe slowdowns or failures.
