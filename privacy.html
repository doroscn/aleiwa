Privacy Policy

At Aleiwa, we respect user privacy and are committed to keeping this website simple, transparent, and secure.

Information We Do Not Collect

Aleiwa does not require user registration and does not collect:

  • Personal identifiable information
  • Account data or login credentials
  • Payment or financial information

Log & Usage Data

Like most websites, Aleiwa may collect limited technical information through standard server logs, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Requested pages
  • Timestamp of access

This information is used solely for operational purposes, including website maintenance, performance monitoring, and abuse prevention.

Cookies

Aleiwa does not use cookies for user tracking or profiling.

Third-party services, such as analytics or advertising providers (when enabled), may use cookies or similar technologies in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

Aleiwa may display third-party advertisements or links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party services.

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: To understand website usage and improve user experience. Google's privacy policy applies to this service.
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google's privacy policy and AdSense program policies apply.

Data Usage

Collected technical data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties except when required to comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you have concerns about our data practices, please contact us.

Children's Privacy

Aleiwa is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in website functionality or regulatory requirements. The latest version will always be available on this page.
